Financial Aid

Financial Aid to Support Indian Speaker travel

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

The PSSI has allocated a total budget of INR 1,20,000 per annum in order to support Indian speakers to deliver their presentations at international conferences.

Following are the guidelines that PSSI will follow while chosing a candidate for granting him/her financial aid:

  1. Financial aid can be availed only once in a lifetime.
  2. His/Her talk should be selected for the conference they are planning to attend.
  3. It will be mostly on a first come first serve basis, but preference will be given to the people applying for the first time.
  4. Maximum financial aid that shall be given to a person will be INR 30,000.
  5. The amount will be deposited into the accepted candidate's account post conference.
